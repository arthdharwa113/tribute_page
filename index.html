CEO of Alphabet Inc. and Google

About Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. Under his leadership, Google has expanded its product range and accelerated innovations. Pichai has been a pivotal force in making technology more accessible and improving the internet experience for billions of users worldwide.

Born in Madurai, India, Pichai earned his degree in Metallurgical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, and later pursued an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. His journey from an engineer to the CEO of one of the world’s largest tech companies is truly inspiring.

Notable Accomplishments

Google Chrome

Led the development of Google Chrome, which has become the world’s most popular web browser.

YouTube

Oversaw the successful acquisition and integration of YouTube, which has significantly grown under Google's wing.

Android

Championed the development of Android, which now powers a vast majority of smartphones globally.

AI and Machine Learning

Played a key role in expanding Google’s reach into AI and machine learning, enhancing products like Google Assistant.

Diversity and Inclusion

Focused on increasing diversity and inclusion within the tech industry.
